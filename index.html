About Me

I am currently a junior studying at MIT. I am majoring in physics and computer science, with a minor in mathematics. I love learning math and using it to solve interesting problems, especially relating to my work and projects.

I am on the squash team at MIT and I have been playing for 12 years. It is my favorite sport and I like traveling with the team!

I also like reading, watching TV, and playing Minecraft.
